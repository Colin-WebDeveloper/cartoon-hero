Monkey D. Luffy

Aspiring King of the Pirates!

All About Luffy

Swept up in the era's passion for piracy, Monkey D. Luffy sets out to become a pirate...but not just any pirate. At a young age, he sets the goal to become King of the Pirates! Helping him in this quest is the power of the Gum-Gum Devil Fruit he accidentally ingests as a child. The fruit instantly transformed him into a rubber person!

Influences

Gol D. Roger

The original King of the Pirates and inspiration for the Golden Pirate Era, at his execution Roger encourages all to take his mantle by finding his ship, the One Piece.

Red-Haired Shanks

A father figure to Luffy, pirate captain Shanks (and his crew) befriended adolescent Luffy and even saved his life. Shanks is the source of the Gum Gum Devil Fruit Luffy ingests and the straw hat Luffy cherishes.

Portgas D. Ace

Luffy's older brother Ace is a powerful pirate and crew member of Blackbeard's priate ship. Ace's dominance is aided by his Flame-Flame Devil Fruit power.

The Gum-Gum Devil Fruit

The source of Luffy's strength and fortitude, this devil fruit turns a human body into rubber making Luffy impervious to all puncture wounds and adding a rubberized solution set to every challenge he faces. He is only weakened when immersed in water.

Friends/Crew

Roronoa Zoro

1st Appears: Season 1 Episode 2

Characteristics: Bushido sword fighting expert with great strength, discipline, and stamina. Usually wears a white shirt and traditional haramaki, sometimes with a mask. He has green hair and constantly clashes with Sanji (who calls him "moss head").

Treasure: Seeks the skills to become the greatest sword fighter.

Usopp

1st Appears: Season 1 Episode 4

Characteristics: Cowardly inclinations but can be ingenious in a tight situation. Inventor of gadgets—usually silly—and teller of tale tales. Long nose, goggles, longish curly hair. Usually wears brown overalls. Carries a slingshot and a big shoulder bag.

Treasure: Seeks fame as a pirate to follow in his father's footsteps. (Yassop is a member of Shanks's crew.)

TonyTony Chopper

1st Appears: Season 1 Episode ?

Characteristics: A blue-nosed talking reindeer who ate a Human-Human devil fruit. He has "roan-style" powers allowing him to transform into several powerful variant forms. Usually wears a top hat and pants. He is a trained doctor.

Treasure: Wants to experience the world and become a respected doctor.

Nami

1st Appears: Season 1 Episode 1

Characteristics: Deeply gifted cartographer, navigator, and sailor, Nami is also a master thief turned pirate. She's also a fashion maven who changes her look two or three times per season. She had to overcome trust issues brought on by a childhood of captivity and servitude.

Treasure: Money, gold, and jewels. Nami always has "berries" shining in her eyes.

Sanji

1st Appears: Season 1 Episode ?

Characteristics: Tall, blonde, with curly eyebrows, Sanji is a talented chef dedicated to serving nutritious meals to folks at sea. Unusually strong, Sanji has developed a kicking fighting style to preserve his hands. He smokes and has a debonair cool attitude—unless girls are around. That's when he turns completely lovesick. He constantly clashes with Zoro.

Treasure: Girls and cooking.

Nico Robin

1st Appears: Season 2 Episode ? ("Baroque Works")

Characteristics: A statuesque beauty with an Aquiline nose, Robin is an introverted bookworm due to her childhood hiding from the law. Robin has been a fugitive since ingesting the Flower-Flower devil fruit at 8 years old. Her devil power is to sprout appendages (usually hands) in great profusion on the surface of any object. Robin is a brilliant archaeologist (and perhaps a Manchurian Candidate-style agent). Robin always looks like a runway model. She favors purple and cowboy hats and always looks cool!

Treasure: Seeks the panegyra or panegloss: an ancient war machine (I think)
